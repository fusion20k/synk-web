Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Synk pricing and features

What's the difference between Free and Pro? �
Free plan lets you sync 1 Google Calendar and 1 Notion database with manual sync. Pro plan includes unlimited connections, automatic live sync, manual/incremental sync options, and upcoming AI integration for just $4.99/month.
Can I try Pro before purchasing? �
You can use the Free plan to test Synk features. When you're ready for more connections and automatic syncing, upgrade to Pro at any time.
How does pricing work? �
Free plan costs nothing and stays free forever. Pro plan is $4.99/month with a 20% discount if you pay annually ($49.90/year). You can cancel anytime, no commitment required.
Can I cancel anytime? �
Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. Your service will continue until the end of your current billing period.
What happens to my data if I cancel? �
Your data remains in your Notion and Google Calendar accounts. Synk only facilitates synchronization and doesn't store your content permanently.
Do you offer refunds? �
Yes, we offer a 30-day money-back guarantee. Contact support for assistance with refunds.
Is my data secure? �
Absolutely. We use OAuth authentication, encrypt all data in transit, and follow industry best practices for security. We never store your passwords.
