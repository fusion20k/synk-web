Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Synk pricing and features

What's the difference between Free and Pro? →
Free plan lets you sync 1 Google Calendar and 1 Notion database with manual sync. Pro plan includes unlimited connections, all sync modes (manual, automatic, incremental), and upcoming AI integration for $14.99/month.
Can I try Pro before purchasing? →
Yes! Pro monthly subscriptions include a 7-day free trial. You can test all Pro features including unlimited connections, automatic sync, and incremental sync risk-free. Cancel anytime during the trial with no charge.
How does pricing work? →
Free plan costs nothing and stays free forever. Pro plan is $14.99/month (with 7-day free trial) or $149.99/year (save $30 annually). You can cancel anytime, no commitment required.
Can I cancel anytime? →
Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. Your service will continue until the end of your current billing period.
What happens to my data if I cancel? →
Your data remains in your Notion and Google Calendar accounts. Synk only facilitates synchronization and doesn't store your content permanently.
Is my data secure? →
Absolutely. We use OAuth authentication, encrypt all data in transit, and follow industry best practices for security. We never store your passwords.
