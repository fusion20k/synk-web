Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Synk pricing and features

How does the free trial work? ›
Your 14-day free trial begins automatically when you connect both Notion and Google Calendar. No credit card required to start. You'll have full access to all features during the trial.
What happens after my trial ends? ›
After your trial ends, you'll need to subscribe to continue using Synk. Your sync configurations will be saved, and you can resume immediately upon subscription.
What's the difference between Pro and Ultimate? ›
Pro includes all core sync features perfect for individuals and small teams. Ultimate adds advanced features like custom field mapping, advanced filtering conditions, bulk operations, detailed analytics, and configuration export/import for power users.
Can I cancel anytime? ›
Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. Your service will continue until the end of your current billing period.
What happens to my data if I cancel? ›
Your data remains in your Notion and Google Calendar accounts. Synk only facilitates synchronization and doesn't store your content permanently.
Do you offer refunds? ›
Yes, we offer a 30-day money-back guarantee. Contact support for assistance with refunds.
Is my data secure? ›
Absolutely. We use OAuth authentication, encrypt all data in transit, and follow industry best practices for security. We never store your passwords.
