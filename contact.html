Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about Synk

How secure is my data? →
Synk uses OAuth 2.0 authentication and never stores your passwords. We only access the minimum data necessary for synchronization and use industry-standard encryption.
Which Notion properties can be synced? →
You can sync titles, dates, descriptions, status properties, and custom fields. Synk intelligently maps these to appropriate calendar event fields.
Can I sync multiple calendars? →
Yes! You can connect multiple Google Calendars and map different Notion databases to different calendars based on your workflow needs.
What happens if I make changes in both places? →
Synk has intelligent conflict resolution that handles simultaneous changes. You can configure how conflicts should be resolved in your settings.
Is there a free trial? →
Yes! Synk offers a 14-day free trial for the Pro plan so you can test the synchronization with your actual Notion and Google Calendar data before committing.
How often does sync happen? →
Synk provides real-time synchronization for most changes, with background sync every few minutes to ensure everything stays perfectly aligned.
