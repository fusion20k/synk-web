Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about Synk

How secure is my data? →
Synk uses OAuth 2.0 authentication and never stores your passwords. We only access the minimum data necessary for synchronization and use industry-standard encryption.
Which Notion properties can be synced? →
Synk syncs task titles, dates, and other key properties from your Notion databases to Google Calendar events. Changes to these properties automatically update in both platforms.
Can I sync multiple calendars? →
The Free plan includes 1 Google Calendar and 1 Notion database. Pro plan users get unlimited Google Calendars and Notion databases, allowing you to map different databases to different calendars based on your workflow needs.
What happens if I make changes in both places? →
Synk uses two-way sync to keep both platforms updated. Changes made in Notion appear in Google Calendar, and changes in Google Calendar sync back to Notion automatically.
Is Synk free? →
Yes! Synk is free forever with no credit card required. Connect Notion and Google Calendar and start syncing immediately. Pro features are available for users who need advanced functionality.
How often does sync happen? →
Synk offers both manual and automatic sync modes. Automatic sync runs regularly to keep your Notion and Google Calendar in sync. Pro users also get incremental sync for even faster updates.
