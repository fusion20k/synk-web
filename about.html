How Synk Works

Discover how easy it is to connect your Notion workspace with Google Calendar for seamless productivity.

Simple Setup, Powerful Results

Get your productivity tools working together in minutes

1

Connect Your Notion Account

Start by securely connecting your Notion workspace using OAuth 2.0 authentication. This ensures your credentials remain safe while giving Synk the necessary permissions to read your selected pages and databases.

  • Click "Connect Notion" in the Synk app
  • Authorize Synk in your browser
  • Select which workspaces to include
  • Your connection is established securely
2

Link Google Calendar

Connect your Google Calendar using Google's secure OAuth system. Synk will only access the calendars you specify, ensuring your privacy while enabling seamless synchronization.

  • Click "Connect Google Calendar"
  • Sign in with your Google account
  • Choose which calendars to sync
  • Grant necessary permissions
3

Configure Your Sync Settings

Take full control over what gets synchronized. Choose specific Notion databases, pages, or properties that should appear in your calendar. Set up custom mapping rules and sync preferences.

  • Select Notion databases to sync
  • Choose which properties map to calendar events
  • Set sync frequency and direction
  • Configure notification preferences
4

Enjoy Automatic Synchronization

Once configured, Synk works in the background to keep your tools perfectly aligned. Changes in Notion appear in Google Calendar instantly, and calendar updates can flow back to Notion based on your settings.

  • Real-time bidirectional sync
  • Automatic conflict resolution
  • Background processing
  • Detailed sync logs and status
