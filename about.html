How Synk Works

Discover how easy it is to connect your Notion workspace with Google Calendar for seamless productivity.

Simple Setup, Powerful Results

Get your productivity tools working together in minutes

1

Download & Install

Download Synk for Windows and install it on your computer. The app is lightweight and installs in seconds—no complicated setup required.

  • Download from our website
  • Run the installer
  • Launch Synk
  • Start free with 1 calendar and 1 database
2

Connect Your Accounts

Securely link both Notion and Google Calendar using OAuth 2.0. We never see your passwords—all authentication happens directly through Notion and Google's secure systems.

  • Click "Connect Notion" and authorize in your browser
  • Click "Connect Google Calendar" and grant permissions
  • Both connections are encrypted and secure
  • You control what Synk can access
3

Select What to Sync

Choose which Google Calendars and Notion Databases should stay in sync. With the free plan, pick your most important calendar and database. Upgrade to Pro for unlimited connections.

  • Select your Google Calendar(s)
  • Choose which Notion database(s) to sync
  • Set your preferred sync mode (manual, incremental, or automatic)
  • Customize sync direction and filters
4

Stay Synchronized

Once configured, Synk keeps everything in perfect harmony. Pro users enjoy automatic live sync—changes appear instantly across both platforms. Free users can manually trigger syncs whenever needed.

  • Automatic sync with Pro (changes appear instantly)
  • Manual sync with Free (on-demand synchronization)
  • Incremental updates to save time
  • View sync status and logs anytime
